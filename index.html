All We Can Save

Why are we doing this?

A lot of us have climate change on our radar, but find it complicated to get involved or start learning.

It's a topic can be intimidating, or feel unfortunately trendy.

But there's literally something for everyone when it comes to caring about "the climate", and approaches abound: science, spiritualism, activism, art, urban planning, history, etc.

What

We're hoping to make a low-key event for learning and discussion. Not something that takes the topic casually, but not something perscriptive. Just a space with the minimum structure for people to stay still something that provides miniumum structure necessary for people to stay in touch on this issue.

Something communal, interesting, reliable: chat about climate change and eat crepes together once every-other month.

That's it. If other things bloom out of this (activism, field trips, art project) then cool! If not, at least we ate crepes.

We'll use the book, All We Can Save, as a common touchstone to get the conversation started. But we'll take it slow! We encourage you to read the last chapter of the book and/or listen to this podcast.
