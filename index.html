All We Can Save

Why are we doing this?

A lot of us are looking for a hopeful focus. A sustained purpose for our energies. Something to shape the constant triage of stimuli and emergencies that funnel toward us each day. Something to prevent privileged optimism from becoming privileged nihilism.

A lot of us have come to sense—if not know—that, in the long run, a fever pitch is unsustainable. But what can replace it?

Maybe we should slow down. Should search quietly for the intersection of our interests, our abilities.
